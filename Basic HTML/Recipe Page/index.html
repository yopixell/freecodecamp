How to Create Fried Egg

Fried is a great choice to make simple dish, itu easy and delicious if you eat with warm rice, in this page you will know how to cook fried egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Oil or Margarine
  • Salt
  • Water or Ice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the stove and frying pan, then heat some oil or margarine.
  2. Prepare a bowl and crack 2 eggs into it.
  3. Add salt, then beat the eggs until well mixed.
  4. Once the pan is hot, pour in the beaten eggs.
  5. When the bottom is cooked, add a little water or ice on top of the eggs, then cover the pan.
  6. Once fully cooked, remove from the pan and the omelet is ready to serve.
